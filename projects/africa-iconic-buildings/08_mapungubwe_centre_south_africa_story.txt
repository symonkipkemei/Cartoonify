The Mapungubwe Interpretation Centre won World Building of the Year for using a vaulting technique that ancient Africans invented, North Africans perfected, Gaudi made famous, and a South African architect later "rediscovered" — a journey that neatly traces the entire arc of whose knowledge gets credited. The centre tells the story of a sophisticated pre-colonial civilisation; the entry fee costs more than the average Limpopo daily wage.
